Because of the terrorist acts in date of 07 jan 2015, you can now show your support to Charlie Hebdo by putting a ribbon that says "#JeSuisCharlie" on your WordPress site.

== Description ==

A black ribbon with the words "#JeSuisCharlie" will be put on in the top right corner of your website, linking to the [twitter hashtag](http://twitter.com/#jesuischarlie). Look at the screenshots to see how the ribbon looks. Thank you for showing your support.

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin zip archive.
2. Extract it in your `wp-content/plugins` folder or upload via admin panel.
3. Browse to your plugins section and active the plugin.

== Screenshots ==
1. This ribbon will appear on your websiten and will link to the twitter hashtag search.
